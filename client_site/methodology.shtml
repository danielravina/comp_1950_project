Methodology

Analysis | Design | Implementation | Deployment & Review

1 Analysis

  • Understanding the Client Expectations
  • Establish Requirements - a review of the existing site - advantages and disadvantages.
  • Establishing Requirements - understanding what we wanted and how we wanted to change.
  • Proposals - what we wanted and did not want in the design.
  • Team member contribution of ideas
  • Understanding individual contributions
  • Understanding what could be implemented within the working timeframe.

2 Design

  • Wireframing
  • Design - several excellent page designs reviewed by team
  • Design - discussion of features, fonts, colors.
  • Organization - setting work environment for the group - Github, Google Drive and Tumblr
  • Prototype and concurrent testing

3 Implementation

  • Coding HTML CSS JavaScript
  • Developing Content
  • Testing and Revision

4 Deployment & Review

  • Completion of content and code.
  • Final test and revision
  • Publishing to Host
  • Review of final product in live environment.
